Work Experience



Supply Chain Reliability Engineering Intern - Summer 2023

I was part of the Starlink Aviation team responsible for integrating antennas onto aircraft to provide in-flight high-speed, low-latency internet. I consulted with the avionics engineers from Falcon 9 to incorporate their harness testing practices into the Starlink department. Additionally, I developed new testing procedures and acceptance requirements to ensure the aviation harnesses met both our needs and FAA standards.



Manufacturing Engineering Intern - Fall 2022

I worked in a new product integration lab for the Hybrid Aircraft Demonstration program. The objective of the program is to enhance the efficiency of aircraft engines by embedding electric motor/generators in turbofan engines, optimizing performance during various phases of flight. During my time there, I assembled multiple subassemblies for high-voltage power converters by referencing engineering drawings and CAD models. I have gained a deep understanding of the importance of design for manufacturing and have developed detailed work instructions to assist in future assemblies.



Process Design Engineering Intern - Summer 2022

I worked on a high-volume production line that manufactures Electronic Control Units (ECUs) for General Motors vehicles. In a lean manufacturing environment, I effectively reduced downtime by addressing and eliminating recurring machine crashes, resulting in savings of thousands of dollars each production day
